Blog

I write a bi-monthly blog called The Nineteenth Century Nerd which aims to make nineteenth-century history, literature, and art interesting and relatable to avid-readers and non-readers alike. Over 2021 I aim to cover topics such as railway history, popular literature (including GCSE and ALevel syllabus texts), art and design, archives and museums, and material culture.

With my blog I hope to spark interest in Victorian history, a period that young people are often deterred from after studying Dickens at GCSE! By opening up the humour, oddities, and relatable parts of the nineteenth-century I hope to help people reconnect with it! Head over to my blog site to check out all of my posts or click here to read my latest post!
