Cross Stitch

For a bit of escapism I have taken to cross stitch! I try to adapt patterns and make my own designs, seeing what works and what might need to go back to the drawing board. Check out some of my hoops and designs below!
Cross stitch is as therapeutic as it is aesthetically pleasing, and it's a great tool for switching off and being creative. In time I hope to create more and more unique designs that I can share with others.
