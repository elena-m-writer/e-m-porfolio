Hi, I'm Elena...

I'm a writer, blogger, and creator.

Over the past five years I've completed a BA in English and Drama, worked in digital marketing, achieved a distinction in MA English Studies, started a blog, learnt basic coding, and read a lot of books. I have also volunteered for my local museum, engaged more with wildlife, and started learning about typography and design. It's been a busy few years!

Being qualified in English and passionate about the humanities, it's no suprise that I love researching and writing. I am particularly interested in historical literature and spent my MA exploring and reconsidering how nineteenth-century texts used language, structure, and style to capture the new age.

If there's one thing I'm interested in, it's words.

I have learnt how to use words to capture a message, idea, or feeling. With this power words can then inform, engage, and encourage. When writing my blog, I aim to use words to transform historical texts and information into relatable, humorous, and engaging content, to encourage readers to be as fascinated by the Nineteenth Century as I am!

If you'd like to check out more of my writing head to my writing page or head straight to my blog, The Nineteenth Century Nerd, for all sorts of nineteenth-century literature, history, and culture!

I've created this site to share more of my skills and what I get up to, from new cross stitch patterns to learning about type. Feel free to have a browse and get in touch if you'd like to know more.
